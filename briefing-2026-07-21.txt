The episode emphasized the importance of addressing these factors to take control of pain and improve overall well-being. As they put it, pain is a whole person problem that requires a whole person solution. This means that as an individual, you need to consider not just the physical aspects of your pain, but also the psychological and social factors that may be contributing to it.

The Hidden Brain episode also discussed the use of pacing protocols and seeking professional help to address pain. This is a really important point, because it highlights the need for a comprehensive approach to pain management that takes into account all of the factors that may be contributing to your pain. By working with a healthcare provider and using a pacing protocol, you can gradually increase your activity levels and reduce your pain over time.
